Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Simple Counter ("the App") handles your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data (counter values) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses AppsFlyer SDK for attribution analytics. AppsFlyer may collect anonymized device information (such as device type, OS version, and IP address) for the purpose of measuring app install attribution. No personally identifiable information is collected. For more details, please refer to AppsFlyer's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
